What is Droom?

Droom is the next step in the evolution of job search and career placement. Leveraging technology and job placement experience seamlessly into one integrated platform where both job seekers and employers can maximize their time and increase the efficiency all in one.

For Job Seekers

Droom will allow job seekers to create a rich profile detailing their work history and skillset. After creating your profile you will be able to search all of the available jobs matching your search criteria. If you’re in a job just swipe right. If it’s a mutual match both parties will be able to communicate freely.

For Employers

Droom allows employers to create a company profile and post job opportunities. Once your job is posted you can begin viewing profile cards to find your job candidates. Profile cards give a brief summary of skills and work history allowing you to quickly find the right person for the job. When a match is made, you will be able to message the user regarding the job opportunity.
